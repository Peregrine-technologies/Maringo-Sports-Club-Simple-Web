Maringo Sports Club

Maringo Sports Club was formed with the intention of developing talent among youths within Maringo County in various games.

Our club offers a variety of games such as swimming, hockey, lawn tennis, basketball, football, and more.

We enroll youth between the ages of 12 and 35 years. Both individual and group registration is allowed, with the option to join through schools, colleges, or religious organizations.

The membership fee is KSh 1,000 for individuals and KSh 500 for group members. The members are categorized into three age groups: 12-17 years (minors), 18-25 years (middle), and 26-35 years (seniors).
